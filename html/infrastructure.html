Infrastructure :

Since its inception in 1990, NIRB is located at the sprawling campus of Regional Institute of Co-operative Management in Bengaluru, the garden city of India.

The Institute has created its own facilities of furnished air-conditioned class rooms with audio visual aids to meet the training needs. In 2011, NIRB established a fully air-conditioned , state of the art computer laboratory with 31 systems, connectivity, LAN and provision for CBS End Users (application) programmes.

In addition to the above, NIRB utilizes RICM infrastructure including library, recreation facilities, hostel and mess which meet the necessary standards for conducting the training programmes.
