Advisory Services :

We provide advisory services to organisations engaged in rural banking. A number of advisory assignments have been completed so far. Study report on revitalisation of Karnataka State Cooperative Apex Bank has been completed recently. Similarly, an assignment to prepare an appropriate business model for the future operations and development of Karnataka State Agriculture & Rural Development Bank has also been successfully undertaken recently. A study of a DCCB for development & achieving organisational excellence is on the anvil.
