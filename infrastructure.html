Infrastructure

Situated at the expansive Regional Institute of Cooperative Management in South Bangalore, NIRB offers class rooms, recreational facilities, hostels, and dining facilities.

The institute has well furnished, air-conditioned classrooms with modern audio-visual aids, accommodating dual channels for training sessions.

Additionally, a state-of-the-art air-conditioned Computer Lab with 31 systems caters to banking technology and computer operations.

Transport facilities are available for field exposure and practical orientation, fostering an ideal learning environment.
