International Exchange and Study Visits

✶ Since 2000, NIRB has orchestrated numerous reciprocal international study visits in partnership with the National Union of Cooperative Banks in Poland. In addition to these exchanges, a study visit was organized for Directors of the Karnataka State Coop. Federation to Malaysia, Singapore, and Hong Kong.

✶ NIRB actively collaborates with organizations such as ICA-AP, ICBA, RCBA & COBI to conduct various national and international events, including meetings, seminars, and conferences.

✶ Recently, in January 2010, delegates from the Rural Development Bank of Sri Lanka attended a specially curated presentation on the "Role of Rural Development Banks in India and Staff Help Groups" at our institute.
