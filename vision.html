The Vision

Excellence in Rural Banking through training and development of human resources.

The Mission

To impart training, education and undertake consultancy on a continuing basis for achieving professionalism in Management and Operations, develop human resources in the Rural Financing Institutions (RFIs) in general and Cooperative Banks and RRBs in particular.

Objectives

To facilitate quality training, nurture operational and management skills, undertake study, research and extend consultancy services to RFIs of moderate costs.
